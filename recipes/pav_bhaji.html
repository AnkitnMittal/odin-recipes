Ingredients

  • Pav Bhaji Masala
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Add potatoes, cauliflower, peas & carrots to pressure cooker with water
  2. Mash the contents after 1-2 minutes
  3. Heat a pan with oil, add chopped onions, ginger, chilli, capsicum & finally tomatoes
  4. Mix well and fry for 2-3 minutes. Add water for consistency as needed
  5. Add coriander leaves or lemon juice according to taste
  6. Heat a pan with oil & toast Pav evenly on both sides
  7. Add butter wherever necessary to enhance the taste
  8. Serve hot
