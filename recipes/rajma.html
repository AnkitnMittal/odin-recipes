Ingredients

  • Rajma
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Soak the Rajma overnight in 4 to 5 cups water
  2. Add dried rajma to pressure cooker with water and salt
  3. While Rajma is cooking, add masala, finely chopped onions and pureed tomatoes
  4. Cook for 7 to 8 mins and then add spices
  5. Stir and cook spices to prepare the masala
  6. Release pressure from cooker and add prepared masala
  7. Now, pressure cook again for 10 minutes and add salt as needed
  8. Serve hot
