Ingredients

Steps

  1. Grind the Masala ingredients into a coarse powder.
  2. Fry the onions until golder brown followed by tomatoes
  3. Grind the mixture to form gravy
  4. Add gravy with vegetables as suited followed by spices
  5. Add paneer at the end and cook for 5 minutes
  6. Serve hot
